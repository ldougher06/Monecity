Monecity has what you need!

Nashville-based Monecity is a virtual payment processing company, which has the nation's only municipal virtual payment service called "VEEP."

Monecity serves municipal and state governments by competitively securing optimal payment processing relationships for cities and other governmental bodies.

Services

What do we do?

  • Reduce overhead expenses
  • Create an additional revenue stream
  • Modernize an organization's Accounts Payable department

How do we do it?

  • Eliminate paper checks
  • Integrate a virtual payment solution specific to an organization's needs
  • Strategic partnerships with companies specializing in electronic payments

What are the benefits?

  • Immediate cost savings
  • Added revenue stream
  • 1% in monthly rebate for all AP payments converted to the virtual platform
  • Reduces check fraud exposure

Virtual Payment Processing:

Like checks, but better.

Monecity is the first and only virtual payment processing company to serve municipal governments by delivering monthly revenues back to cities for VEEP processed payments made by the city to its vendors.

Because its service is compensated by the four payment processing companies, not by Metro Nashville, Monecity provides VEEP at no cost to the city!
